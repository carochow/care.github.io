meet Carolyn

My training and coaching is driven by a philosophy to train smart, train hard. This involves designing strength training programs that keep in mind injury prevention, while putting in the work when you need to. I have always had a passion for fitness, playing multiple sports for 20 years. Some of my experiences in sports include: climbing, specifically bouldering, for 6 years and playing volleyball for over 12 years. As a lifelong athlete, I believe that everyone is an athlete. We can all strength train and benefit from its advantages.
I completed a Fitness & Health Promotion diploma from Humber College in 2017. Since then, I have been professionally training individuals and groups working towards targeted strength goals. In addition, I developed and coached volleyball and climbing training sessions.
